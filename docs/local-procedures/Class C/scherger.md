## Airspace
When **SG TCU** is online, R604A, R604B, R605A, and R605B are activated. This equates to Class C & Class G airspace within 40 DME YBSG from `SFC` to `F245`.

Various airspace modes may be activated around YBSG by SG TCU, depending on the operations planned. The most common is [TRA Scherger](#tra-scherger). Rarely, [SG MIL CTR](#military-control-zone) may be activated.

### TRA Scherger
When **SG TCU** is online, **TRA Scherger** is activated by default. The airspace extends from `SFC` to `A040` within 15NM DME YBSG, clipped to the southwest.

The activation status of TRA Scherger will be reflected in the ATIS.

### Military Control Zone
The **SG MIL CTR** (Military Control Zone) is defined as airspace within 15NM DME YBSG, `SFC-A040`, clipped to the northeast. The SG TMA is unique in that the associated MIL CTR is not necessarily activated by default when **SG TCU** is online. When the SG MIL CTR is activated the entire area is reclassified as Class C airspace.

The activation status of the SG MIL CTR will be reflected in the ATIS.

### Tower Closed Procedures
When **SG TCU** is offline, the restricted airspace `F125` and below reverts to Class G. CTAF procedures apply at YBSG and [YBWP](./weipa.md).

## Circuits
The circuit height is `A015`.

### Circuit Direction
| Runway | Direction |
| ------ | ----------|
| 30     | Right     |
| 12     | Left      |

## Departures
VFR aircraft should expect to depart via a visual departure, on track to their first tracking point.

IFR aircraft shall expect to depart via a [pilot-managed](../../navigation/ifrdepartures.md#other-departure-methods) IFR departure or **visual departure**.

## Arrivals
An RNP approach is available to both runways.

IFR aircraft can generally expect to be processed direct to the IAF for the following approach:

| Runway | Approach |
| --- | --- |
| RWY 12 | RNP |
| RWY 30 | RNP |

VFR aircraft can expect to join the circuit for the most suitable runway.

### Initial and Pitch
The intial points are aligned with Taxiway A at the following locations.

| RWY  | Initial Point | Altitude |
| ---- | ------------- | --------------------------- |
| 12   | Bend in Mission River at 4.5 TAC SGR | `A025` |
| 30   | Intersection of Marmoss Creek and easterly estuary | `A025` |

## Special Use Airspace
### Military Gates
There are several military gates established throughout the SG TMA to facilitate entry and exit to adjoining SUA.

| Intended SUA    | TCU Exit Gate       |
| --------------- | ------------------- |
| N/A (Deps East) | Gate 2, 4, and 6    |
| M601A           | Gate 8, 10, and 12  |

!!! note
    Details of each gate can be found in the [Scherger FIHA](https://ais-af.airforce.gov.au/australian-aip).

!!! phraseology
    *FBED14 plans to enter the M610A MOA via the Gate 10 for military training and airwork.*  
    **FBED14**: "Scherger Ground, FBED14 for Gate 10, `F180` for M610A, request clearance."  
    **SG SMC**: "FBED14, Scherger Ground. cleared Gate 10 direct, climb to `F180`, squawk 6001, departure frequency 124.2."   
	
## Helicopter Operations
### Helipads
There are two helipads at YBSG, both located south of Taxiway A2.

Both helipads are outside of the manoeuvring area so no takeoff or landing clearances will be issued. Instead, helicopters will be instructed to report airborne or report on the ground.

!!! phraseology 
    **SG ADC**: "RHWK11, Pad 1, report on the ground"
