## Airspace
**ED ADC** administers the ED MIL CTR airspace from `SFC` to `A015`. **AD TCU** administers the overlying airspace from `A015` to `F245`.

### Tower Closed Procedures
When **ED ADC** is offline, ED MIL CTR (Class C `SFC` to `A015`) reverts to Class G. CTAF procedures apply at YPED.

## Departures
VFR aircraft should expect a visual departure on track to their first tracking point.

IFR aircraft should expect to be issued with a SID as per below:

| Aircraft Type | Runway | First Waypoint | SID |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| All | All | DOLVU | DOLVU SID |
| All | All | All others | ED (RADAR) SID |

## Arrivals
An ILS and RNP approach is available to RWY 18.

IFR aircraft can generally expect to be processed via a STAR for the following approach:

| Runway | Approach |
| --- | --- |
| RWY 18 | ILS |
| RWY 36 | Visual |

### Initial and Pitch
The intial points are east of the runway, at the following locations. Aircraft will pitch west in order not to impede on the Class G boundary. 

| RWY  | Initial Point | Altitude |
| ---- | ------------- | -------- |
| 18   | 2NM South     | `A015`   |
| 36   | 4NM North     | `A015`   | 

## Special Use Airspace
SUA exists in the surrounding enroute airspace, which pilots may request the activation and use of. Pilots must make any such requests to SMC when requesting airways clearance.

## Circuits
Circuit operations occur within the CTR bounded by the Gawler River to the north and the powerline running from the Gawler River (1.5NM south of the Gawler Township) to the south eastern boundary of the ED CTR.

## Helicopter Operations
### Helipads
There is one helipad at YPED, on Taxiway D immediately east of OLA 11.

The helipad is part of the manoeuvring area. Any helicopter taking off or landing on the helipad requires a specific takeoff or landing clearance from ADC.

!!! phraseology 
    **ED ADC**: "CHOP41, taxiway D, cleared to land"
