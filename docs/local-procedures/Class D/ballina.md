## Airspace
BA ADC is responsible for the Class D airspace in the BA CTR from `SFC` to `A015`.

### Tower Closed Proceedures
When BA ADC is closed, the Class D airspace from `SFC` to `A045` is reclassified as Class G.

## Departures
VFR aircraft should expect to depart via a visual departure, on track to their first tracking point.

IFR aircraft should expect to be issued with a SID as per below:

| Aircraft Type | Runway | First Waypoint | SID |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Jets | All | IDNER | IDNER SID |
| Non-Jets | RWY 06 | BUVAM | BUVAM SID\* |

All other aircraft shall expect the BA (RADAR) SID. A visual departure may be available in lieu of a SID during day VMC.

!!! note
  \* The BUVAM SID is not available when M641 Evans Head is active.

## Arrivals
RNP and RNP(AR) approaches are available to both runways. A NDB approach is available to RWY 24.

IFR aircraft can generally expect to be processed via a STAR (from the south) or direct to the IAF (from the north) for the most suitable approach.

## Circuits
All circuits are to be flown at `A010`.

| Runway   | Day  | Night |
| ---------| -----| ----- |
| RWY 06   | Left | -     |
| RWY 24   | Left | Right |
